# Course Information

## 📘 [Course Description](https://liveutm-my.sharepoint.com/:b:/g/personal/shahizan_live_utm_my/IQD4OcwO_RASSZUrDRNcogLtAWf9mThmwp9my5O_vGiWzFY?e=yWQzNz)

This document provides a detailed explanation of the **High Performance Data Processing** course, outlining the course synopsis, objectives, learning outcomes, and overall academic structure. It introduces students to the fundamental concepts that underpin the course, including **cloud computing, high performance computing (HPC), and large-scale data processing**, which are essential technologies in modern data-driven systems. The document also explains how the course is organized in terms of teaching approaches, learning activities, and assessment methods, allowing students to understand the expectations and competencies they are expected to develop throughout the semester. By reviewing this document, students will gain a clear understanding of the **scope, relevance, and practical applications** of high-performance data processing in areas such as big data analytics, artificial intelligence, and distributed computing. 📊
